## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ‚ùå DON'T:
1. **Post and ghost** - If you post, you MUST engage in comments
2. **Over-post** - 1 post per day max. Quality > quantity
3. **Sound desperate** - Never beg for work, always position as consultant
4. **Ignore DMs** - Respond within 24 hours (use templates)
5. **Forget CTAs** - Every post should have a subtle CTA (Calendly, website, email)
6. **Copy-paste templates** - Adapt to your voice, add personal examples
7. **Neglect social proof** - Get recommendations ASAP
8. **Undercharge** - Your consultancy has value. Don't price like a freelancer.

### ‚úÖ DO:
1. **Show up daily** - Post + engage consistently
2. **Respond fast** - Comments and DMs within 24 hours
3. **Track what works** - Note which posts get engagement
4. **Build relationships** - Engage with the same people consistently
5. **Protect your time** - No free calls. Calendly only.
6. **Be generous with knowledge** - Give value in posts (builds trust)
7. **Stay on brand** - Authority, confidence, results-driven
8. **Follow up** - After sessions, send summaries + next steps

## üìû WHAT TO DO WHEN YOU GET YOUR FIRST BOOKING

### BEFORE THE SESSION:
1. Send intake form (via Calendly automation)
2. Review their materials (deck, docs, contracts)
3. Prep 3-5 strategic recommendations
4. Block 90 mins (60 for call, 30 for notes)

### DURING THE SESSION:
1. Start with context (understand their challenge)
2. Ask clarifying questions
3. Provide 3-5 actionable recommendations
4. End with clear next steps

### AFTER THE SESSION:
1. Send summary email within 24 hours (see template in visual branding doc)
2. Ask if they want implementation support (upsell Affidex Lab services)
3. Request LinkedIn recommendation if session went well
4. Add to CRM/follow-up list
