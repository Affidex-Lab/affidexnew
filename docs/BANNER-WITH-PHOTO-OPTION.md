## STEP-BY-STEP: CREATING OPTION A IN CANVA

### STEP 1: PREPARE YOUR PHOTO (5 MINS)
Before opening Canva, prepare your headshot:

**Option 1: Use Remove.bg**
1. Go to remove.bg
2. Upload your headshot
3. Download with transparent background
4. Save as "headshot-transparent.png"

**Option 2: Use Canva's Background Remover**
1. Upload photo to Canva first
2. Click "Edit photo"
3. Click "BG Remover" (may need Canva Pro)
4. Remove background

**Photo Tips:**
- Use a recent professional headshot
- Face clearly visible, good lighting
- Neutral or solid background (easier to remove)
- Facing camera or slightly angled
- Confident, approachable expression

---

### STEP 2: CREATE BANNER IN CANVA (20 MINS)

**1. Set Up Canvas**
- Go to Canva.com
- Click "Create a design" â†’ "Custom size"
- Enter: 1584 x 396 px
- Click "Create new design"

**2. Add Background**
- In left sidebar, click "Elements"
- Search "black background"
- Drag to fill entire canvas
- Ensure background is pure black #000000

**3. Add Your Photo**
- Click "Uploads" in left sidebar
- Click "Upload media"
- Upload your headshot
- Drag photo onto canvas

**4. Make Photo Circular**
- Click on your photo
- Click "Crop" at top
- Select "Circle" crop shape
- Adjust to frame your face nicely
- Click "Done"

**5. Resize & Position Photo**
- Resize photo to approximately 220 x 220 px
- Position: 150px from left edge
- Vertically center it (use alignment guides)

**6. Add White Border to Photo**
- With photo selected, click "Border" at top
- Choose white color
- Set border weight to 3-5px
- This creates the circle outline

**7. Optional: Add Glow Effect**
- Duplicate your photo circle
- Place duplicate behind original
- Slightly enlarge duplicate (230 x 230 px)
- Change color to gold #FFC107
- Set transparency to 20%
- This creates subtle glow

**8. Add Main Text (Statement)**
- Click "Text" â†’ "Add a heading"
- Type: "I DON'T DO INTERVIEWS."
- Position: Start around 450px from left edge
- Font: Inter Bold or Poppins Bold
- Size: 26px
- Color: White #FFFFFF
- Character spacing: 3
- All caps
- Left-aligned

**9. Add Second Line**
- Duplicate text box
- Change to: "I SOLVE PROBLEMS."
- Position: 20px below first line
- Keep same styling

**10. Add Gold Line**
- Click "Elements" â†’ Search "line"
- Choose thin horizontal line
- Color: Gold #FFC107
- Width: 150px
- Thickness: 2px
- Position: Left-aligned with text, 30px below statement

**11. Add Your Name**
- Add new text box
- Type: "Utibe Samuel"
- Font: Inter Semi-bold
- Size: 15px
- Color: White #FFFFFF
- Position: 20px below gold line

**12. Add Title**

- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 12px
- Color: Light gray #B0BEC5
- Position: Directly below name

**13. Add Services Line**

- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 11px
