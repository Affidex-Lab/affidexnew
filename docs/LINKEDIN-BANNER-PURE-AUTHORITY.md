## REMOVING THE "HIRING" BADGE

### STEP-BY-STEP:

**Method 1:**
1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
2. Click your profile photo
3. Click "Frames" at the bottom
4. Select "None"
5. Click "Apply"

**Method 2:**
1. Click the pencil icon on your profile photo
2. Under "Profile photo frames"
3. Select "None"
4. Save changes

**Method 3 (Remove "Open to Work" entirely):**
1. Click "Open to" button at top of profile
2. Deselect all options
3. Save

This removes the green circle frame completely.

## UPLOADING YOUR BANNER
