## PART 1: LINKEDIN VISUAL BRANDING

### PROFILE PHOTO SPECIFICATIONS

**Technical Requirements:**
- **Size:** 400 x 400 px (min), 7680 x 4320 px (max)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 8 MB

**Style Guidelines:**
- **Background:** Clean, professional (solid color or subtle blur)
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders, facing camera
- **Expression:** Confident, approachable (no forced smile, no stern)
- **Attire:** Business casual or smart casual (avoid hoodies unless that's your brand)
- **Lighting:** Well-lit, no harsh shadows
- **Consistency:** Use the same photo across LinkedIn, X, website

**Pro Tip:** Get a professional headshot if possible. It's a $100-300 investment that signals seriousness.
